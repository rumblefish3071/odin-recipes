Pineapple peach smoothie

pineapple peach smoothie is a wonderfully refreshing beverage.

This Peach Pineapple Smoothie is a delicious smoothie that can be made with fresh, frozen, or canned fruits and is perfect for breakfast or a snack.

The Ingredients you will need.

  • 1 can of pineapples
  • 1 can of peaches
  • ice

    • That is all you will need

  • To make a peach pineapple smoothie you will simply combine your peaches, pineapple, juice, and ice in a blender and then blend until smooth. Serve it right away while still cold.

I am using a Ninja blender a very good blender for smoothies.

  1. In a high power blender, place all the ingredients in the blender. Blend for 45 seconds on high or until the fruit and ice are smooth.
  2. Divide evenly between two glasses and garnish with additional pineapple if desired.
