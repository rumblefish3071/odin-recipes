Banana bread

Mom's mouth watering banana bread

Use the Overripe bananas in your fruit basket and make Banana Bread!

Banana bread is a semi-sweet bread made by smashing overripe bananas and combining it with a cake batter. Most of the breadâ€™s taste comes from the overripened bananas, so it's a little healthier than other sweet baked goods but I think banana bread has such delious flavors.

The Ingredients you will need.

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cups flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 3 to 4 overripe Bananas

Steps to a delious banana bread

  1. Beat overripe bananas (electric beater works great)
  2. beat 2 eggs
  3. Mix dry Ingredients
  4. Add eggs and bananas to dry mixture
  5. mix until batter is smooth
  6. butter sides of a loaf pan
  7. pour mixture into loaf pan
  8. pre heat oven to 350 degrees
  9. cook banana bread for 25 to 30 min
  10. Remove from over let sit fir 10 min and enjoy
